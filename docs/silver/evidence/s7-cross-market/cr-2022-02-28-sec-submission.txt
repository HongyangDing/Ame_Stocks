<td align="left" style="vertical-align:top"> <p style=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold;text-align:left">Entry Into a Material Definitive Agreement </p></td></tr></table> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On February&#160;28, 2022, Crane Co., a Delaware corporation (&#8220;Crane Co.&#8221;), Crane Holdings, Co., a Delaware corporation and wholly-owned subsidiary of Crane Co. (&#8220;Crane Holdings&#8221;), and Crane Transaction Company, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company and wholly-owned subsidiary of Crane Holdings (&#8220;Merger Sub&#8221;), entered into an Agreement and Plan of Merger (the &#8220;Reorganization Agreement&#8221;), pursuant to which Crane Co. will merge with Merger Sub, with Crane Co. surviving the merger as a wholly-owned subsidiary of Crane Holdings (the &#8220;Reorganization Merger&#8221;). The Reorganization Merger is subject to specific conditions, including approval by Crane Co.&#8217;s stockholders at its 2022 Annual Meeting of Stockholders (the &#8220;Annual Meeting&#8221;), which is currently scheduled for April&#160;25, 2022, and the Reorganization Merger will not be consummated until after such conditions are satisfied or, to the extent possible, waived. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Following the Reorganization Merger, Crane Holdings intends for Crane Co. (which will, as of that time, be a wholly-owned subsidiary of Crane Holdings) to convert from a Delaware corporation to a Delaware limited liability company (such conversion, together with the Reorganization Merger, the &#8220;Reorganization&#8221;). </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Under the Reorganization Agreement, at the effective time of the Reorganization Merger, all of the issued and outstanding shares of Crane Co. common stock, par value $1.00 per share (&#8220;Crane Co. Common Stock&#8221;), other than treasury shares, will be converted automatically on a <span style="white-space:nowrap"><span style="white-space:nowrap">one-for-one</span></span> basis into shares of Crane Holdings common stock, par value $1.00 per share (&#8220;Crane Holdings Common Stock&#8221;), and, as a result, the current stockholders of Crane Co. automatically will become stockholders of Crane Holdings, holding the same number and percentage of shares of Crane Holdings Common Stock as they held of Crane Co. Common Stock as of immediately prior to the Reorganization Merger. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In addition, under the Reorganization Agreement, (i)&#160;each outstanding option to purchase shares of Crane Co. Common Stock, if not exercised before the completion of the Reorganization Merger, will be adjusted automatically into an option to acquire, at the same exercise price, an identical number of shares of Crane Holdings Common Stock and (ii)&#160;each outstanding restricted share unit, performance-based restricted share unit and deferred stock unit, in each case, for shares of Crane Co.
